## üîç Why Perplexity Might Not Show Your Site Yet

Perplexity doesn't have direct submission - it relies on:
1. **Being indexed by Google/Bing first** (primary source)
2. **Having backlinks** from other indexed sites
3. **Fresh, quality content** that answers questions
4. **Social signals** (mentions on social media)

**Action:** Submit to Google Search Console FIRST. Perplexity will follow within days.
